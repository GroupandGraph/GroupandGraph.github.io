Prime graphs and actions of primitive groups

Melissa Lee

University of Auckland

Time: 16:00-18:00 (GMT+8), Tuesday May 26th, 2022
Location: Tencent Meeting


Abstract: I will be discussing a number of problems related to properties of finite groups that have piqued my interest over the past couple of years. Many involve bases of permutation groups \(G\leqslant\mathrm{Sym}(\Omega)\), which are subsets \(B\subseteq\Omega\) with trivial pointwise stabiliser. There will also be a few open problems along the way.

This seminar was also advertised by SUSTech International Center for Mathematics for its Algebra and Discrete Mathematics Colloquium. See the poster and the webpage.


Host: 李才恒 Cai Heng Li
