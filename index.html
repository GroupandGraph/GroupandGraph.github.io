这是群论方向的系列报告，我们希望能够促进国内外青年学者（博士生为主）在群论研究上的交流。报告主题分布在（但不限于）以下研究方向：

- 有限单群（有限单群分类定理，子群结构，共轭类等）
- 置换群及其在组合学上的应用（如传递图）
- 线性代数群与有限李型群
- 有限域与群的表示
- 有限\(p\)-群
- 计算群论
我们欢迎更多的科研工作者参与到此系列报告中，分享最新的研究成果，课题综述，技巧方法等。如果您有意愿做报告，或者想为我们推荐报告人，请联系黄弘毅（邮箱hy.huang@bristol.ac.uk）。

This is a platform for young group theorists (mostly PhD students) to talk about their research and related topics. The topic can be anything related to group theory, including but not limited to the following.

- Finite simple groups (CFSG, subgroup structures and conjugacy classes)
- Permutation groups and their applications to combinatorics (e.g. transitive graphs)
- Linear algebraic groups and finite groups of Lie type
- Finite fields and group representations
- Finite \(p\)-groups
- Computational methods in group theory
If you are a young group theorist and want to give a talk, please drop an email to Hong Yi Huang at hy.huang@bristol.ac.uk. We would also love to hear any suggestions you have for speakers. Don't worry if you have no new results -- we also warmly welcome any survey, or even a method that you find helpful, on any related topic.

Time

一般地，系列报告定期于北京时间每周一下午16:00-18:00进行，包含一小时报告与一小时自由讨论时间。受地区限制，目前报告形式为线上或线下线上同时进行。

Our usual time is Monday 16:00-18:00 (GMT+8). This comprises a session of a 1-hour seminar talk, and another 1-hour free discussion on any math or non-math topic. The seminar is currently held online or in hybrid style.

The Next Seminar

Title: A classification of finite groups with three automorphism orbits

Speaker: 朱砚舟 Yanzhou Zhu (SUSTech)

Time: 16:00-18:00 (GMT+8), Monday December 19th

Location: Tencent Meeting 768 228 5095

Abstract: Denote by \(\omega(G)\) the number of orbits of the action of \(\Aut(G)\) on group \(G\). It is well-known that \(\omega(G)=2\) if and only if \(G\) is an elementary abelian \(p\)-group. In this talk, I will give a classification of finite groups \(G\) with \(\omega(G)=3\).
